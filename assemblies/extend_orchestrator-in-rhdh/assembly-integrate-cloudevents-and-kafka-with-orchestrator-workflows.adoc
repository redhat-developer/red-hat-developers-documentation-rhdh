= Integrate CloudEvents and Kafka with Orchestrator workflows

:context: integrate-cloudevents-and-kafka-with-orchestrator-workflows

[role="_abstract"]
Configure Apache Kafka connectivity in {product-short} Orchestrator to trigger workflows by using CloudEvents and to integrate event-driven systems and message brokers with your organization's workflow.

With event-driven workflow execution, you can integrate {product-short} workflows with existing enterprise messaging infrastructure. Use this feature to trigger workflows asynchronously and maintain loose coupling between services.
