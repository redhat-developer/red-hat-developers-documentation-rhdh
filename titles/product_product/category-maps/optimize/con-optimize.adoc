= Optimize

[role="_abstract"]
Improve {product-short} performance, reliability, and resource efficiency to support growing user demand, maintain responsive operations, and ensure the platform scales effectively as your development organization expands.

== Why optimize {product-short}

As your development organization adopts {product-short} and usage grows, optimizing platform performance becomes critical to maintaining developer productivity and platform reliability. Without optimization:

* Page load times degrade during peak usage hours
* Resource consumption increases unnecessarily
* Platform performance becomes unpredictable under load
* Scaling operations take longer than necessary
* User experience deteriorates as traffic grows

Optimization ensures {product-short} remains responsive, efficient, and reliable regardless of scale, traffic patterns, or concurrent user activity.

== What you can optimize

{product-short} optimization focuses on performance scaling, resource efficiency, and operational reliability:

Performance scaling::
Configure high availability architecture, enable caching mechanisms, and implement horizontal scaling to handle growing traffic while maintaining consistent response times.

Resource efficiency::
Optimize resource allocation, reduce unnecessary overhead, and implement efficient storage patterns to minimize infrastructure costs while supporting platform growth.

Operational reliability::
Establish monitoring practices, implement health checks, and configure automated recovery mechanisms to maintain platform availability and prevent service degradation.

== How to approach optimization

Effective optimization follows a systematic approach:

. *Assess current performance* by monitoring response times, resource utilization, and traffic patterns to establish baselines and identify bottlenecks.

. *Plan scaling strategy* based on your organization's size, growth trajectory, and expected traffic to determine appropriate replica counts and resource allocations.

. *Implement performance improvements* through high availability configuration, caching enablement, and resource tuning to address identified bottlenecks.

. *Monitor and adjust* by continuously tracking performance metrics and adjusting configurations as traffic patterns and usage evolve.

Optimization is an ongoing process—monitor platform performance after each change to validate improvements and identify new optimization opportunities as your organization grows.
