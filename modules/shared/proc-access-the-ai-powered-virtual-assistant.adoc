= Access the AI-powered virtual assistant

[role="_abstract"]
You can use the AI-powered virtual assistant ({ls-short}) in {product} to answer questions, debug issues, and guide you through tasks using natural language. The virtual assistant appears as a floating action button on the home page.

.Prerequisites

* {ls-short} is {installing-and-configuring-lightspeed-book-link}#install-and-configure_installing-and-configuring-lightspeed[installed and configured] in your {product} instance.
* You are logged in to your {product} instance.

.Procedure

. Navigate to the home page in your {product} instance.
. Locate the *Lightspeed* floating action button on the page.

.Verification

. Verify that the *Lightspeed* floating action button appears on the page.
. Click the *Lightspeed* floating action button to open {ls-short}.
+
The virtual assistant opens in your last saved display mode, which can be overlay, drawer, or full page, or defaults to overlay mode.
. Enter a question to confirm that the virtual assistant responds with AI-generated answers.
. Click the close icon to close the virtual assistant.
