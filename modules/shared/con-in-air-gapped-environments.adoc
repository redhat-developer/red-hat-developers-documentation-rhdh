= {ls-short} in air-gapped environments

[role="_abstract"]
To provide chat assistance in a network environment without internet access, you must mirror the required {ls-short} container images to your local registry.

This infrastructure process ensures that your secure environment can pull the necessary sidecar images inside your network perimeter.
