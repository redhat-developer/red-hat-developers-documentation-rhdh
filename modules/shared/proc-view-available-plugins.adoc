= View available plugins

[role="_abstract"]
You can view available plugins for your {product} application on the *Extensions* page.

.Procedure

. Open your {product-very-short} application and click *Administration* > *Extensions*.
. Go to the *Catalog* tab to view a list of available plugins and related information.
