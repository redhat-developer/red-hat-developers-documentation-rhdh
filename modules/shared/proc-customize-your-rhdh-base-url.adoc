= Customize your {product} base URL

[role="_abstract"]
Change the default {product} base URL to match your organization's DNS configuration.

.Prerequisites
* You know your required {product-short} external URL: pass:c,a,q[{my-product-url}], and have configured DNS to point to your {ocp-brand-name} cluster.
* {configuring-book-link}[Custom {product-short} configuration].

.Procedure
* In your custom `{my-app-config-file}` file, enter your {product-short} external URL, such as pass:c,a,q[{my-product-url}].
+
.`{my-app-config-file}` excerpt
[source,yaml,subs="+attributes,+quotes"]
----
app:
  baseUrl: {my-product-url}
backend:
  baseUrl: {my-product-url}
  cors:
    origin: {my-product-url}
----
