The `<tag>` variable is your RHDH application{apos}s version of {backstage} and the plugin version, in the format: bs_<backstage-version>_<plugin-version>. To find this information:

. Look in the {product-short} link:X[release notes preface] for your {backstage} version.
. Go to the link:https://github.com/orgs/redhat-developer/packages?tab=packages&q=[plugin packages list in the rhdh-plugin-export-overlays repository]. Search for and select your plugin.
. Locate your {backstage} version in the list of tagged image versions, and you see the complete value to use for the `<tag>` variable.
