= Enabling self-service with Software Templates

To allow developers to create new projects independently using your standards, you must publish the template definition to the {product-name} catalog. This registration makes the template a selectable option in the software creator interface.

.Procedure
. Navigate to the *Create* page in the {product-name} interface.
. Select *Register Component*.
. Enter the full URL to your `template.yaml` file in the *Repository URL* field.
. Click *Analyze* and then *Import*.

.Verification
. Navigate to the *Create* page.
. Confirm that your new template appears in the list of available options.
