= Managing the visibility of the Favorites group

With the *Favorites* group, you can focus on high-value conversations by pinning them to the top of the chat history panel. You can toggle pinned chats from the *Lightspeed* settings dropdown. The *Pinned chats* section is always displayed if the user enables the pinned chats option.

Customize the visibility of the *Favorites* group in the chat history panel to maintain a minimalist workspace.

* You are logged in to {ls-short}.
