= Locating production-ready components in {product-very-short}

To identify existing software building blocks, view source code, or check lifecycle status, use the catalog filters in {product} ({product-very-short}). Filtering the catalog ensures you can locate production-ready components and documentation without performing manual searches across multiple repositories.

.Procedure

. Navigate to the {product-very-short} interface.
. In the left navigation sidebar, click *Catalog*.
. Click the *Kind* filter and select *Component*.
. Optional: Click the *Type* filter to narrow the list to a specific functional category, such as *Library*, *Website*, or *Tool*.
. Enter the component name in the *Search* bar at the top of the *Catalog* page.
. Select the component card from the results to view its *Overview*, *Docs*, and *Relationships*.
