= Onboard to {product}

To begin using {product-very-short}, you must verify your permissions, confirm software entity ownership, and personalize your workspace. This procedure guides you through the initial setup required to locate team services and use self-service capabilities.

.Prerequisites
* You have access to your organization's {product-very-short} instance.
* You have your corporate login credentials.

.Procedure

. Log in and verify your profile: Confirm you are logged in as the correct user and that the platform recognizes your identity.

** *Authenticate*: Access your organization's {product-very-short} URL and log in using your corporate credentials.
** *Check your identity*: Click your avatar or name in the Global Header and select *Settings*. Verify that the *User Entity* matches your corporate identity.
** *Verify ownership*: Navigate to *My Profile* from the user menu. Check the *Ownership* card to make sure it lists the components, APIs, and systems you or your team are responsible for. If this list is empty, you might need to register your ownership in the Software Catalog.
For more information, see {navigate-rhdh-on-your-first-day-link}#log-in-to-rhdh[Log in to {product-very-short}].

. Organize your workspace: To reduce navigation time across the software ecosystem, curate a list of high-priority resources.

** *Star your services*: {navigate-rhdh-on-your-first-day-link}#managing-starred-items-for-quick-access[Locate your frequently used components in the *Catalog* and select the *Add to favourites* (star) icon to add them to your favorites].
** *Access starred items*: Use the *Starred Items* menu in the Global Header to open your favorite resources quickly.
** *Pin the Sidebar*: Use this if you switch frequently between tools. The sidebar is pinned by default. To unpin the sidebar, go to *Settings* and disable *Pin Sidebar*. See {navigate-rhdh-on-your-first-day-link}#customize-interface-settings[Customize your {product-very-short} interface settings].

. Explore production resources: Production instances provide access to live infrastructure data and technical guides required for operational tasks.

** {navigate-rhdh-on-your-first-day-link}#finding-and-viewing-techdocs[*See production documentation]*: Use the *Docs* menu to find production-specific documentation, such as runbooks or deployment guides linked to your components.
** {navigate-rhdh-on-your-first-day-link}#verify-api-contracts-before-integration[*Check production APIs*]: Navigate to the *APIs* page to confirm that production endpoint definitions are accurate and discoverable.
** {navigate-rhdh-on-your-first-day-link}#import-and-use-an-existing-template-for-faster-development[*Test Self-Service templates*]: Click *Create* (+) in the Global Header to examine available Software Templates. Confirm that you have access to the Software Templates required to generate new production services.

* *Learn the interface*: {navigate-rhdh-on-your-first-day-link}#con-navigate-the-rhdh-interface_quickly-gain-access-and-find-what-you-need-in-rhdh[Understand the Global Header and Sidebar tools].
* *Get AI assistance*: If enabled in production, open {ls-short} to ask questions about your new environment. See {navigate-rhdh-on-your-first-day-link}#get-ai-assisted-help[Get AI assisted help for your development tasks].
