= Finding services in {product-very-short}

To identify backend applications and microservices and understand their dependencies, deployment status, and API relationships, use the filters in the {product-very-short} Software Catalog.

.Procedure

. In the left navigation sidebar, click *Catalog*. 
. Click the *Kind* filter and select *Component*. 
. Click the *Type* filter and select *Service*. 
. Optional: Filter the list by selecting a specific *Owner* or *Lifecycle*, such as `production` or `experimental`. 
. Click a service card to open the detail view.
. Review the *Overview* tab for owner information and links to source code. 
. Click the *Dependencies* or *Relations* tab to see upstream and downstream connections.
