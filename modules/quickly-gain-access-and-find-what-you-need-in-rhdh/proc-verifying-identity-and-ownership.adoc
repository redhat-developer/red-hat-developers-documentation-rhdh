= Verifying identity and ownership in {product-very-short}

To verify account details and review software components assigned to you, review your user profile in {product-very-short}.

.Procedure

. In the global header, click your avatar or name.
. Select *My Profile*. 
. Review the *About* card to verify your user details and tags. 
. Examine the *Ownership* card to review software entities linked to your account. 
. Use the toggle switch to change the view between *Direct Relations* and *Aggregated Relations*.
