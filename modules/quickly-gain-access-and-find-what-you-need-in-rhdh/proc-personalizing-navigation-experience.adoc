= Personalizing the navigation experience in {product-very-short}

Customize the {product-very-short} interface to match your preferences. You can adjust the display theme, pin the sidebar, and create shortcuts for frequently accessed resources.

.Prerequisites

* You are logged in to the {product} console.

.Procedure

. Adjust interface settings: 
.. Click your avatar in the global header and select *Settings*. 
.. Toggle *Pin Sidebar* to keep the menu visible or allow it to collapse. 
.. Under *Appearance*, select *Light*, *Dark*, or *Auto* for the Theme.

. Create shortcuts (favorites): 
.. Navigate to a component, API, or documentation page. 
.. Click the *Star* (*) icon near the title. 
.. To access these shortcuts later, click the *Star* icon in the global header.

.Troubleshooting navigation

If you cannot locate a specific resource:

* Use the *Search* field in the Global Header.
* Check the *Catalog* and remove any active filters.
* Click the *Support* button in the Global Header to contact administrators.
