= Discovering global plugins in {product-very-short}

To access features that apply across the entire platform, locate global plugins in the main navigation sidebar of {product-very-short}.

.Procedure

. Log in to your {product-very-short} instance.
. Examine the left navigation sidebar.
. Locate standard or custom items that represent specific extensions, such as:
* *Tech Radar*: Visualizes approved technologies.
* *Cost Insights*: Tracks cloud spending.
* *Developer Lightspeed*: Provides AI-assisted development help.
* *Learning Paths*: Offers training and educational resources.
