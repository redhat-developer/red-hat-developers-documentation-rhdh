= Finding components in {product-very-short}

To find specific software building blocks and view their documentation, source code, or lifecycle status, use the catalog search and filters in {product-very-short}.

.Procedure

. Navigate to the {product-very-short} interface.
. In the left navigation sidebar, click *Catalog*.
. Click the *Kind* filter and select *Component*.
. Optional: Click the *Type* filter to narrow results, such as *Library*, *Website*, or *Tool*.
. Enter the component name in the *Search* bar at the top of the page.
. Click a component card to view its *Overview*, *Docs*, and *Relationships*.
