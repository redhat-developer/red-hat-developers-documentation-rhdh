= Browsing the extensions marketplace in {product-very-short}

If your administrator has enabled the marketplace, you can view a comprehensive list of installed and available plugins for your {product-very-short} instance.

.Procedure

. In the left navigation sidebar, click *Extensions*.
. Browse the marketplace to review the following categories:
* *Installed Plugins*: Integrations currently active in your instance.
* *Available Plugins*: Additional integrations supported by the platform that your team can request.

[NOTE]
====
End users typically cannot install plugins. If you locate a plugin in the marketplace or the Backstage Plugin Directory that your team requires, contact your administrator to request its enablement.
====
