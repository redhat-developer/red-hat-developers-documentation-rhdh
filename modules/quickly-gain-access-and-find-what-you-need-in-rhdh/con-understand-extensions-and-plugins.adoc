= Understand extensions and plugins

To integrate your development toolchain into a single interface, use extensions and plugins in {product} ({product-very-short}). While core features such as the *Software Catalog* and *TechDocs* are pre-installed, you can utilize additional plugins to interact with external services directly from the portal.
