= Locating resources instantly in {product-very-short}

To find specific components or documentation, use the universal search bar in {product}. The search engine offers smart suggestions that update as you type to help you navigate directly to a resource.

.Procedure

. Click the *Search* input field in the global header.
. Enter your query, such as a service name or a concept.
. Review the instant results displayed in the drop-down menu, which include matching components, APIs, documentation, templates, and users.
. Click a suggestion to navigate directly to that resource.
