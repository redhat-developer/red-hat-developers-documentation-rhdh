= Logging in to {product-very-short}

Log in to the {product-very-short} web console to access the platform.

.Procedure 

. Navigate to the Red Hat Developer Hub URL provided by your administrator (for example, `https://developer-hub.yourcompany.com`).
. On the login page, select your authentication method:
* *Enterprise Authentication:* Select this option to log in with your corporate credentials (such as Single Sign-On or Keycloak).
* *OAuth Provider:* Select a provider like GitHub if your organization uses external authentication.
* *Guest Access:* If available, select *Enter as Guest* for read-only exploration. Note that guest access typically has limited permissions.
. Enter your credentials and complete the authentication prompts.

.Verification

* You are redirected to the *Homepage*.
* A personalized "Welcome" greeting appears at the top of the dashboard.
