= Adjusting interface settings in {product-very-short}

To suit your working environment and visual preferences, customize {product-very-short} interface.

.Procedure

. In the Global Header, click your avatar or name to open the user menu. 
. Select *Settings*. 
. In the *Appearance* section, configure the following options: 
* *Theme*: Select *Light*, *Dark*, or *Auto* to sync with system preferences.
* *Language*: Select your interface language.
* *Pin Sidebar*: Toggle this option to keep the left navigation menu permanently visible.
